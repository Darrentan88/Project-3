Axolotls

The axolotl is a paedomorphic salamander. Axolotls are unusual compared to other amphibians in that when they reach adulthood axolotls do not metamorphisis and take to the land. Instead, adults remain aquatic and gilled. The species was originally found in several lakes underlying what is now Mexico City, such as Lake Xochimilco and Lake Chalco.
