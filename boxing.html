Chess Boxing

>Chess boxing is a hybrid sport combining the likes of chess and boxing all at once.

The competitors alternate between chess and boxing with a timed interval for each section

The players win based on if the opponent is either knocked out first or checkmated.

There are two major leagues of chess boxing currently the World Chess Boxing Association (WCBA) and World Chess Boxing Organization (WCBO)

Chessboxing was invented by French comic book artist Enki Bilal and eventually adapted by Dutch performance artist Iepe Rubingh as an art performance Chessboxing has grown into a competitive sport Chessboxing is particularly popular in the United Kingdom, India, Finland, and Russia. Chesboxing is making its ways through America currently although to a smaller audience.
